About Us

The Sports Department at Bennett University is a team of individuals, with multiple ideas but one eventual goal, to provide impetus to sporting culture among students.

The Sports Committee is the backbone of various well- organised events that take place throughout the year. We believe that the values of a sportsperson are just as important as one’s skill set on the field. Therefore, we want every player and non- player to discover the spirit of sports within them.

To promote fitness and to bid adieu to lethargy, students have been given access to these world- class facilities on campus:
1. Indoor air conditioned Squash and Basketball courts.
2. Outdoor Volleyball and basketball courts.
3. Football field with flood lights.
4. Outdoor Synthetic Turf Lawn tennis court.
5. Cricket ground, pitch and practice nets.
6. A fully equipped gymnasium with strength training and cardiovascular equipments.
7. Pool and snooker tables with required equipment.
